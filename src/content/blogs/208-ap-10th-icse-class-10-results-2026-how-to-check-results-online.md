<h2>Results Season 2026: Success for Millions of Students</h2>
<p>The long wait is over for students across Andhra Pradesh and those under the ICSE board. The <strong>AP 10th Class Results 2026</strong> and <strong>ICSE Class 10 Results</strong> have been announced simultaneously today, April 30, 2026.</p>

<h3>How to Check Your AP SSC 10th Results 2026</h3>
<ol>
  <li>Visit the official BSEAP website at <strong>bse.ap.gov.in</strong>.</li>
  <li>Click on the 'SSC Results 2026' link.</li>
  <li>Enter your Hall Ticket Number and Date of Birth.</li>
  <li>Download your marks memo for future reference.</li>
</ol>

<h3>ICSE Class 10 Result Highlights</h3>
<p>The ICSE board has once again recorded an impressive pass percentage. The results are available on the <strong>cisce.org</strong> portal. Schools can also access results through the CAREERS portal using the Principal's login ID and password.</p>
