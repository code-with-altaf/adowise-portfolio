### Step 1: Real-Time Sourcing with Adowise Map

Traditional database providers sell stale, outdated lists compiled months or years ago. For local B2B targets (such as gym facilities, dental practices, retail stores, real estate offices, or business services), the absolute source of truth is **Google Maps**.

With **Adowise Map**, you can input a category and region (like *"Gyms in Ashburn, VA"*) to run a live headless scraper that gathers data in real time:

![Adowise Map populated leads spreadsheet](/adowise-map-screenshot.png)

By extracting validated listings directly from the source, you ensure:
1. **100% Active phone numbers** and verified operational statuses.
2. **Current categories and exact addresses** for local intent.
3. **Identified website assets** (or the lack thereof—which acts as the perfect pitching angle).

### Step 2: Pitching Intent with Adowise AI

Having list records is only half the battle. Standard cold email blasts are instantly deleted. High-performing outbound requires **hyper-personalization** and context. 

This is where **Adowise AI** comes in. Once you've scraped your targets, you feed the data directly into our intent-based AI outreach digital workers. The AI analyzes the business profile, checks their online presence, identifies structural opportunities (like a gym lacking a premium mobile-responsive website or booking flow), and initiates highly compelling multi-channel outreach.

![Adowise AI in action](/adowise-ai-screenshot.png)
*Adowise AI interacting with prospects, qualified leads, and planning booking strategies.*

For example:
* **The No-Website Angle:** *"Hey Mayweather Boxing, I noticed you have 58 excellent Google reviews but no active website listed. You're losing dozens of Ashburn customer signups daily to competitors who offer simple online bookings. Let me build a clean mobile booking flow for you in 3 days."*
* **The High-Intent Follow-up:** If the business replies, Adowise AI handles reply management, qualifying their interest and directing them to your calendar without manual intervention.
